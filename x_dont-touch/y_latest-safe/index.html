Rules and guidelines:

- Be respectful.

- Take the time to proofread your posts if you want people to understand you. (Unfortunately, for some people this rule is not a given.)

- Try to keep your posts lean and helpful. If you see a problem, just solve it, troubleshoot it, or give a piece of advice.

- When you relay a useful piece of information that calls for a citation instead of deductive reasoning or common sense, then simply cite it - author, name of work, exact spot within the work. It will help the strength of your argument on a subject.

All in all, just be approachable. Anything you write will make an impression of you. The list of rules and guidelines is tentative, so expect changes at some point.

- Humboard moderator team
